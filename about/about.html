About PhysicX

PhysicX is a modern physics simulation platform designed to make scientific experimentation, visualization, and learning accessible to everyone.

Platform

What is PhysicX?

PhysicX is a physics simulation platform built to support students, researchers, and engineers in understanding physical systems. It combines real-time visualization, interactive simulations, and scientific tools into one unified environment — bridging the gap between abstract theory and tangible experimentation.

Whether you are studying classical mechanics, electromagnetism, fluid dynamics, or optics, PhysicX provides the interactive canvas to explore, verify, and communicate physical phenomena with precision and clarity.

Mission & Vision

Build a next-generation physics simulation platform that empowers students, researchers, and engineers to explore and innovate through interactive, high-performance tools.
